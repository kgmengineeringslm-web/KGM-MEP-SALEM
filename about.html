About Us

Our Heritage: A Legacy of Excellence Since 1987

The journey of KGM MEP Engineering Services began in 1987, founded by the visionary Late Mr. Murugesan Gopal. What started as a dedicated electrical and plumbing service provider soon grew into a household name across Salem and Yercaud.

Under his leadership, the company specialized in complex Electrical Motor Repairs and Borewell Motor Installations, becoming a trusted partner for residential and industrial clients alike. His deep technical knowledge and commitment to quality laid the foundation for everything we do today.

Growth and Expansion

In 2009, the business expanded into the retail sector with the opening of our Electrical Trade Shops in Gorimedu and Law College Bus Stop. This venture was successfully managed by Mr. Murugesan alongside his wife, Mrs. Lakshmi Murugesan, further strengthening our presence in the local market.

A New Chapter: KGM MEP Engineering Services

Following the passing of our founder in 2024, the company is now headed by Mrs.Lakshmi Murugesan (Proprietor). Today, the legacy continues under the expert guidance of HARIKARAN (Chief Engineer & Manager).

We have evolved into a full-scale MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) service firm. We combine nearly four decades of traditional expertise with modern engineering standards to deliver comprehensive solutions for:

  • Industrial Electrification & Wiring
  • Commercial Building Electrical Systems
  • Advanced Plumbing & Mechanical Installations
  • Specialized Motor & Borewell Services
