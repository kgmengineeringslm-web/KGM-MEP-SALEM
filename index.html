Welcome to KGM MEP Engineering Services

Led by Mr. Harikaran (Technical & Operations Head), we provide top-tier MEP solutions across South India. We ensure safety, quality, and precision in every industrial and residential project we undertake.

Our Heritage: A Legacy of Excellence

The journey of KGM MEP Engineering Services began in 1987, founded by the visionary Late Mr. Murugesan Gopal. What started as a dedicated electrical and plumbing service provider soon grew into a household name across Salem and Yercaud.

Under his leadership, the company specialized in complex Electrical Motor Repairs and Borewell Motor Installations. His deep technical knowledge and commitment to quality laid the foundation for everything we do today.

Growth & New Leadership

In 2009, we expanded into the retail sector in Gorimedu and Law College Bus Stop, managed by Mr. Murugesan and his wife, Mrs. Lakshmi Murugesan.

Following the passing of our founder in 2024, the company is now Proprietored by Mrs. Lakshmi Murugesan and technically led by Mr. Harikaran. We combine four decades of tradition with modern engineering standards.
